About Us

Welcome to the Pictaroo

Pictaroo is an online image sharing community that allows people to both upload their own images, and view
images that other people have chosen to share to the online Pictaroo community. It also allows users to sort images
by categories to view pictaures that suit their interests.

Once registered, users can interact with each other by upvoting or downvoting images, and adding comments to their
own pictures and those shared by others. Users can save their favourite images uploaded by others in order to view them
at a later date.

This website is developed as part of the Internet Technology Group Project
